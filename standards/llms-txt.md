# llms.txt: Making Your Project Discoverable to AI Agents

> llms.txt is a plain-text file at `/.well-known/llms.txt` or `/llms.txt` that tells AI agents and language models what your project does and how to navigate its content efficiently.

The llms.txt standard defines a plain-text file that AI agents can fetch to understand a project's structure without crawling every page. Full coverage including the spec, adoption landscape, and known limitations is in [llms.txt: Spec, Adoption, and Honest Limitations](../geo/llms-txt.md).
