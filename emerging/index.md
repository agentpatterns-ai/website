# Emerging Concepts

> Early-stage ideas worth tracking — not yet established patterns, but too important to ignore.

## Editorial Stance

Content in this section differs from the rest of the site. Patterns and techniques pages require sourced claims and proven applicability. Emerging concepts pages document ideas that:

- Have limited independent sourcing (often a single implementation or author)
- Show potential to become established patterns but lack broad validation
- Challenge existing assumptions about how agent-driven development works

Each page links to its primary sources and notes the maturity level. Concepts that accumulate enough independent evidence graduate to the appropriate section (patterns, techniques, or workflows).
